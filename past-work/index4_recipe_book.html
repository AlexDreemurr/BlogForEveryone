Mapo Tofu

A famous dish from China, well-known by its unique spicy-salty taste and the bouncing taste of tofu.

  • Time: about 40 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity Tips
tofu 500 grams Protein 4%+ tofu
pork meat 200 grams
garlic a half
plant oil enough
Douban sauce 2 spoons choose big-brand ones

Tutorial:

Step 1: Clean the pork meat under water flush.

Step 2: Cut the pork into pieces, and then small chops. Smash the garlic into tiny chops.

Step 3: Take the tofu out of the box carefully, without smashing it. Then cut it into small, arranged blocks(much bigger than pork chops).

Step 4: Add enough amount of water in the pot and set the fire to 'small' to avoid boiling the water. Add tofu blocks to pre-heat the tofu.(This is used to prevent tofu from breaking when cooking in big fire)

Step 5: Now it's time to cook our meal. Add enough amount of oil, set fire to small, and add meat and Douban Sauce. (Only in small fire can the douban sauce's flavor be mixed well in the meal) Keep moving the ingredients in the wok until the meat turned out to be in deep brown.

Step 6: Carefully transmove the tofu with enough water into the wok. Make it to large fire and wait until the water became less enough.

Enjoy your meal!
